For Talent & Representatives

Your HAND ID.
Nobody else’s.

A HAND ID is issued on the basis of your publicly documented, independently verified significance. No employer registers you. No platform creates you. No studio owns you. Your HAND ID belongs to your notability — full stop.

The Problem Today
Your identity lives in someone else’s system.

Right now, every employer, every platform, every streaming service, and every agency has its own internal record for you — with its own ID, its own metadata, and its own terms. When you move on, those records don’t follow you. Your credits get lost. Your likeness gets misattributed. Your residuals get delayed because nobody can agree on who you are.

In the AI era, that fragmentation has consequences that go far beyond inconvenience. If your identity isn’t clearly yours, someone else can use it.

  • ✗Credits don’t follow you when you change representation
  • ✗Residuals delayed because systems can’t agree on who you are
  • ✗Likeness misattributed across platforms with no ground truth
  • ✗Likenesses created and deployed with no provenance record or consent chain
  • ✗Platform shuts down — your record disappears with it
What HAND Changes
One ID. Yours. Forever.

A HAND ID is purely functional — it carries no implication of ownership, employment, or contractual relationship. It identifies the public fact of your notability, not your relationship to any organization.

  • ✓Persists through every career change and employer transition
  • ✓Survives platform shutdowns and data migrations
  • ✓Governed by a nonprofit — not a studio, platform, or agency
  • ✓Every Licensed Likeness permanently linked to you — concurrent at creation or retroactively registered, always traversable
  • ✓Recognized by any DOI®-compliant system worldwide
  • ✓No expiration. No renewal. No transfer of ownership. Ever.

Respeecher
AI Voice Technology
Active Integration
HAND’s Role
HAND IDs provide the authoritative consent record linking a Licensed Likeness (Type 02) to its Legal Person source — ensuring every synthetic voice, AI-generated performance, or authorized vocal permutation can be traced to a verified, consenting talent record. Best practice is concurrent registration: the HAND ID is issued at the moment the likeness is created, so consent and instantiation are co-recorded as a single provenance event. Retroactive registration is fully supported for voices already in production. In both cases, the chain of custody to the NIL rights holder is permanent, traversable, and technically enforceable under AB 1836, the NO FAKES Act, and SAG-AFTRA’s AI provisions.
What Respeecher Does
Leading AI voice cloning platform used in major Hollywood productions — high-fidelity synthetic voice generation for film, TV, gaming, and accessibility applications.
Film productions Voice artists Studios Legal & compliance Guild AI consent

Built for the Whole Industry

The value of a universal identifier standard is directly proportional to how many organizations adopt it. HAND is designed to be accessible to independent producers, boutique agencies, regional leagues, and small guilds — not just major studios and global platforms.

🔓
Lookup is always free
Any person, organization, or system anywhere in the world can search the HAND registry and resolve any HAND ID — at no cost, forever. This is not a freemium hook. It is a foundational commitment to the public utility of the standard. The identifier infrastructure is public. Full stop.
⚖

HAND membership tiers are designed so that a boutique talent agency in Nashville and a major Hollywood studio can both participate meaningfully. The standard works for everyone, or it doesn’t work at all. Broad adoption is the mission — not just enterprise adoption.
Structural Neutrality

Governed by no one.
Trusted by everyone.

HAND is a Delaware non-stock nonprofit C corporation, currently applying for IRS 501(c)(6) trade association status. Not owned by any studio, platform, streaming service, guild, league, or player association. This structural neutrality is the feature — not an oversight.

🎬
Studios & Platforms trust it
Because no guild or player association controls the standard. Adoption doesn’t concede anything to the other side of the negotiating table.
✊
Guilds & Unions trust it

Because no single league, association, or broadcast partner controls the standard. A HAND ID issued to a D1 athlete belongs to that athlete — not to the school, the conference, or the NCAA.
